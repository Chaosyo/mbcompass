MBCompass is a free offline compass and map app.

Works completely without internet once maps are downloaded.

Features:
* Offline vector maps (no internet needed)
* Offline raster tile maps
* Saves favourite waypoints on your device
* No tracking or analytics
* No Google account needed
* Material 3 design with dark mode

How to use offline maps:
1. Download a .map file from download.mapsforge.org
2. Copy it to your phone storage
3. Open MBCompass and select the map
